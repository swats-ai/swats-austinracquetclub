Our Vision

Where Sport Meets
Sophistication

Austin Racquet Club is a world-class racquet sports and wellness destination designed for those who expect the extraordinary. Combining the explosive growth of padel and pickleball with luxury amenities, ARC redefines what a modern athletic club can be.

Designed by acclaimed architect José Luis Hernández Bañuelos, every detail — from our glass-enclosed courts to our spa with living green walls — reflects a commitment to excellence, community, and well-being.
